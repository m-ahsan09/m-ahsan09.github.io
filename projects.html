SCADA-based Condition Monitoring of Wind Turbines

IDUB Postdoctoral Research Project (2025-2026)

Funder: AGH University of Krakow (Excellence Initiative - Research University)

Development of an integrated framework for SCADA-based condition monitoring and fault detection of wind turbines. The project incorporates advanced image analysis and trend monitoring techniques to optimize renewable energy reliability.

Outcome: Publication in Energies (Q1, IF: 3.2): "Multi-Channel SCADA-Based Image-Driven Power Prediction..."
Vehicle Diagnostics using AI and Digital Signal Processing

Doctoral Research Project (2020-2024)

Funder: Polish Government Scholarship + Silesian University of Technology

Utilized low-cost MEMS accelerometers to capture vibration data from vehicle engines. Recorded data was analyzed using advanced digital signal processing and AI models to accurately diagnose engine health conditions.

Outcome: Doctoral Dissertation defended 07/2025; results published in Archives of Acoustics and Vibrations in Physical Systems.
Fault Diagnosis using Advanced Machine Learning

Young Scientist Research Grant (2023)

Project Code: 02/050/BKM23/0036 | Funder: Silesian University of Technology

Research focused on condition monitoring and fault diagnosis using state-of-the-art machine learning methods, specifically targeting variable speed rotating machines and diverse fault depth datasets.

Outcome: Led to multiple peer-reviewed publications and presentations at IEEE SPA 2023/2024 and OSA 2024.
Filter Design for Non-Stationary Signal Analysis

Young Scientist Research Grant (2021)

Project Code: 02/050/BKM21/0021 | Funder: Silesian University of Technology

Investigation into filter design using evolutionary algorithms for estimation in applications involving non-stationary signal analysis. This research enhances early-stage fault detection in rotating element bearings.

Outcome: Publication in IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement (Q1, IF: 5.9).
