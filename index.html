Muhammad Ahsan

Hi, I’m Muhammad Ahsan

Doctoral Candidate in the discipline:
Control, Electronics and Electrical Engineering

I received my Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from Air University, Islamabad, Pakistan, in 2013, and M.Sc. in Control Theory and Control Engineering from Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China, in 2020.

In October 2020, I joined the Silesian University of Technology, Gliwice, Poland, where I have been actively engaged in multidisciplinary research at the intersection of signal processing, machine learning, and control engineering. My work focuses on developing innovative solutions for machine health diagnosis and advanced control systems, leveraging cutting-edge digital signal processing techniques, artificial intelligence methods, and control strategies.

I have authored over 20 publications in reputed journals and conferences, contributing to the advancement of fault diagnosis in bearings, machine health monitoring, and control system design. My research aims to improve the reliability, safety, and efficiency of industrial systems, with a particular emphasis on optimization, nonlinear control, and robust multidisciplinary approaches. Through my work, I continue to push the boundaries of knowledge, making significant contributions to both academia and industry.
